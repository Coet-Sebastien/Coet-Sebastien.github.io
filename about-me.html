Do you know that most web browsers have integrated dev tools ?

  1. Try pressing F12
  2. Now you have access to the dev tools.
  3. Here, you can directly inspect the HTML code, the console, etc (event the stylesheet in Firefox).
  4. On the upper left corner of the dev tools, you can see a symbol with a square and a cursor: try it.
  5. Now, go back to the main page and try to find the hidden part with this. To do that, you just need to delete "visibility:hidden" for the article "hidden-info".
